'M' is for a manic desire.\
It's a dream, a craving,\
That burns the eternal pyre,\
Crackling, cackling, an unholy choir\
\
\
'A' is for an anxious dream,\
For a manic fire\
Must burn its seer\
Burn its seer with fright\
Shivering fright that steals\
Steals the warmth of the light\
\
\
'D' is for dreading the dark,\
A fear of running out,\
Out of wood\
Cold, exposed, stark\
No match,\
No spark\
\
\
What beauty is it to be mad,\
To burn within an eternal cycle,\
With the pyre flames dancing,\
Dancing with passion and fright,\
Delicious dread in the flickering light\
