A picture to paint, of thorns,\
Coiled on a sternum of all,\
With the thorns digging in,\
Bleeding through a heart\
\
\
A picture changes, each morn,\
A different wound, the same clot\
A new thorn, to adorn\
A new picture, still a red blot\
\
\
But a picture remains, still\
Filled with thorns, still\
Around this chest, still\
Who thought only cuts could kill?\
