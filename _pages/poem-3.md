Welcome to the maize maze!

Don't delay, don't linger, don't stay —

For the exits close at dusk,

And we keep no leftovers older than a day!
Welcome to the maize maze!

Breathe in the country aroma,

Breathe out that individual persona —

To escape you need to conform.
Welcome to the maize maze!

So many ways out —

the same illusion.

A different door. Same devout.
Welcome —

Conform. Despair. Beware.

If you get out —

what will be left,

You — a husk?
